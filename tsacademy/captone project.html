How Planetary Data Helps Us
Understand Space

Planetary science goes beyond images. Comparing mass, diameter,
gravity, and density, we gain insight into how planets form, behave,
and interact within the solar system.

Visualizing the Differences Between Planets

Each planet in our solar system has unique physical characteristics. Visual comparisons help highlight how
vastly different terrestrial planets are from gas giants and ice giants.
